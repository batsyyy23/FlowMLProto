FlowML Studio

Component	Technology			Reasons
Frontend	Reflex (formerly Pynecone)	Pure Python, but creates a high-performance React frontend. Scalable & beautiful.
Backend		FastAPI				Built-into Reflex. Handles the heavy lifting.
ML Engine	FLAML				Microsoft's lightweight engine. Perfect for your 3050 (4GB VRAM).
Queue		Redis				Mandatory. Prevents the app from crashing if 2 users click "Train" at once.
Database	SQLite				Simple, single-file database. Easy to backup.
